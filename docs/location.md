# Santa Fe, NM

Santa Fe is the oldest capital city in the US, founded in 1610, and the highest US capital at 7,000 feet. Santa Fe is the historic hub of the southwest connecting the Santa Fe Trail with the Old Spanish Trail, and El Camino Real de Tierra Adentro National Historic Trails. It is renowned for its iconic Pueblo-style adobe architecture, vibrant, multi-cultural art scene, historic Plaza, and over 280 days of annual sunshine. For more information, please see [https://www.santafe.org/](https://www.santafe.org/).
