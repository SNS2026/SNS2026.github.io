Located in the heart of downtown Santa Fe, our historic hotel is housed in a 350-year-old hacienda. We’re a five-minute walk from Santa Fe Plaza, the Georgia O’Keefe Museum, and the New Mexico Museum of Art. Featuring traditional Southwest décor and details throughout, there’s even a kiva fireplace next to our outdoor pool and hot tub.
