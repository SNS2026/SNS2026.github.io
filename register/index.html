    Registration

    The conference will take place over five days and offers a full program of talks, poster sessions, networking opportunities, and social events.

    All presenters must register to attend.

    Registration Period¶

    • Registration: March 15, 2026 - August 24, 2026 (11:59 pm MT)
    • On-site Registration: August 24, 2026 - August 28, 2026

    Registration will remain open until the conference is full.

    Registration Fee¶

    Registration Fee: $XX (USD)

    The registration fee includes:

    • Participation in Scientific Program (Oral Presenations & Poster Sessions)

    • Breakfast and Lunch

    • Coffee/Tea Breaks, Refreshments

    • Conference Banquet

    Accompanying persons are welcome to attend the Welcome Reception and Coffee/Tea Breaks. To attend the conference banquet, there is an additional fee $XX per accompanying person.

    Register for SNS 2026¶

    We encourage all participants to register early, especially international participants to allow for visa processing times.

    When registering please indicate if you will need documents to support your visa application. We can provide a letter of invitation, overview of the conference, itinerary in the United States.

    Information you will need to complete the registration process

    • Name and information of participant
    • Need for visa application supporting documents
    • Number of accompanying persons to attend the conference banquet
    • Interest in visiting Los Alamos National Laboratory and/or the National High Magnetic Field Laboratory: Pulsed Field Facility
